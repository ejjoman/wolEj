Welcome

Wake On Lan is a tool wich allows you to wake up your LAN-Clients (PC, Server, Media Center, ...) in an easy way. It allows you to configure an unlimited number of LAN-Clients. Furthermore you can wake up your configured LAN-Clients directly from your Launcher-Screen or in your own Scripts using the "Copy wake-up-command".

Currently the following languages are supported: English and German.

This app is released under the terms of the GPL v3 and the sources can be found on the project-page on GitHub: https://github.com/ejjoman/wolEj
